ABOUT

I’m a UX designer and front-end developer crafting responsive and functional experiences that help people GET IT DONE! I've worked for start-up agencies, mid-size companies, and freelanced remotely from Mexico City.

My path towards UX can be viewed as a Choose-your-own-adventure book where I've chosen many wrong paths along the way. But experiencing, learning how to overcome, and living through these unique scenarios has strengthened my grit and shaped the way I approach design, and life in general.

My design approach:

  • Drive with curiosity
  • Design for an effortless, enjoyable experience.
  • Make it better than before.
  • Leave a lasting impression.
  • Go for it!

My experience in front-end development has given me a broader understanding of how important it is to listen and empathize with my team as much as I would with the people I'm designing for.

My knowledge of UX and the design process has equipped me with skills to design user-centered products that simplify the task, solve the problem, and enhance the quality of life.
